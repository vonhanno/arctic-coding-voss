The Journey

From Idea to Shipped

Stop prototyping, start shipping. In 3 days you'll go from idea to a live product - step by step with expert guidance.

Step 1

Build

You've played around with vibe coding. Now go deeper. Structure your idea, build a real prototype, and get it ready for the next level. From scattered prompts to a solid foundation.

Step 2

Iterate

Test and improve your ideas. Get feedback from fellow builders, refine your concept -then learn to use Claude Code and develop your prototype into a real product.

Step 3

Ship

Deploy your app to the world with Claude Code. Polish, launch, and leave the retreat with something real and live.

The Outcome

What You'll Walk Away With

A Live Product

Your idea, built and deployed on the internet. Not a prototype, not a mockup. A real, working app.

The Skills to Keep Building

Go beyond prompting. Learn to use Claude Code to develop, iterate, and ship on your own.

A Network of Builders

3 days with people who think like you. Connections that last beyond the retreat.

Momentum

Break through the gap between "I've tried AI tools" and "I shipped something real."

Your Base Camp

Arctic Coliving
Voss Mjølfjell Lodge

Built as a peace project by students in 1939, the Mjølfjell Lodge sits in the Raundalen valley between the epic fjords of Sogn and Hardanger in western Norway.

Coworking Space

Desks, external monitors, and fast WiFi for ~20 people. Purpose-built for focused work.

Shared Rooms

Comfortable shared rooms at the lodge. Private rooms available for an additional charge.

All Meals Included

Breakfast, lunch, and dinner prepared by the in-house chef. Fuel for building.

Mountain Access

Hiking, skiing, and exploring. 30 minutes from Voss resort. Nature at your doorstep.

On-Site Amenities

Sauna, pizza oven, coffee roastery, and a dedicated events space.

Easy Travel

Own train stop at Mjølfjell. 2 hours from Bergen. Airport shuttles arranged.
